Welcome to TimeWizard

Seamlessly Track & Manage Your Time Online .

Features

1. Real-Time Timer Display: Keep track of the time spent on any website with a live timer, updated every second. The timer is displayed prominently, ensuring you always know how long you've been on a site.
2. Custom Time Alerts: Set a specific time limit for any website, and TimeWizard will notify you once you've reached your set time. This feature helps you avoid spending too much time on distracting sites.
3. User-Friendly Controls: Easily set and reset the timer with the integrated buttons. The extension's expandable view allows for a larger control panel, providing a more detailed interface when needed.
4. Persistent Data Storage: TimeWizard saves your time data locally, ensuring that your tracked time is not lost even if you close the browser. The timer will continue from where you left off on the same website the next time.
5. Drag-and-Drop Interface: Move the timer display to any position on your screen for convenience. The floating widget is designed to stay out of your way while you browse.
6. Consistent and modern Styling: Enjoy a polished, uniform look across all websites. TimeWizard's buttons and timer are styled to appear consistently, regardless of the site you visit.
